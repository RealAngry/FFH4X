About Us

FFH4X ARMY is a Free Fire guild owned by Priyansh Gaming with about 5.2k subscribers on YouTube. Join our community of passionate gamers and improve your skills.

Live Schedule

Priyansh Gaming is live every day at 2:30 PM. Subscribe to our YouTube channel to get notified.

We play 4 v 4 matches with our subscribers' squad and also participate in guild wars. We compete in both national and international matches.

Join the Guild

To join the FFH4X ARMY, you have to pass a test of 1 vs 3 against our guild players. Show your skills and become a part of our elite team.

Join our Discord to play 4 v 4 off stream and take the guild test off stream. Our Discord is managed by Angry and owned by Priyansh Gaming.

Community & Tournaments

We organize and participate in tournaments for 4 v 4 and 1 v 1 matches. Stay connected and join our community for regular updates. Being a member of our guild not only helps you improve your gameplay but also provides opportunities to make new friends, participate in exclusive events, and learn from experienced players.

Gameplay Tips

  • Practice Headshots: Regularly practice aiming for headshots to increase your kill rate.
  • Communication: Use voice chat effectively to communicate with your team during matches.
  • Map Knowledge: Learn the map layouts to better anticipate enemy movements and find advantageous positions.
  • Stay Updated: Keep up with game updates and changes to stay competitive.
  • Watch and Learn: Watch streams and videos of top players to understand their strategies and techniques.

How to Become a Successful Free Fire YouTuber

  • Consistency: Upload videos regularly to keep your audience engaged.
  • Quality Content: Focus on creating high-quality videos with good editing, clear audio, and engaging commentary.
  • Unique Style: Develop your unique style or niche, whether it's gameplay, tutorials, or funny moments.
  • Engage with Your Audience: Respond to comments, interact with your viewers on social media, and build a community around your channel.
  • Learn from Others: Watch other successful YouTubers to understand what works and adapt those strategies to your content.
